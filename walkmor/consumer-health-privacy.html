Consumer Health Privacy Notice

Last updated: 1 March 2026

This notice describes how WalkMor collects, uses, and protects consumer health data as required by certain U.S. state privacy laws. It supplements the WalkMor Privacy Policy.

Who We Are

WalkMor is provided by Koi Ventures in Victoria, Australia.

What Is Consumer Health Data

"Consumer health data" means personal information that is linked or reasonably linkable to you and that identifies your physical or mental health status.

Consumer Health Data We Collect

  • Profile and body measurements: height, weight, goal weight
  • Activity data: steps, active calories, exercise minutes, stand hours, distance
  • Nutrition and hydration data: food logs, macros, water intake
  • Coaching check-ins related to hunger, energy, and adherence
  • Health data from Apple Health with your permission

Sources of Consumer Health Data

  • Directly from you when you enter data or log activities
  • From Apple Health when you enable Health permissions
  • From your device sensors (such as step tracking)

How We Use Consumer Health Data

  • Provide walking plans, goals, and progress tracking
  • Personalize recommendations and reminders
  • Improve product performance and reliability
  • Provide customer support

Sharing of Consumer Health Data

We do not sell your consumer health data. We do not share consumer health data with third parties for their own advertising or marketing purposes.

How We Protect Consumer Health Data

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to:

  • Access consumer health data we have about you
  • Request deletion of consumer health data
  • Withdraw consent to collect or share consumer health data
  • Receive a copy of consumer health data in a usable format

How to Exercise Your Rights

To submit a request, contact us at the email below. We will verify your request and respond within the time required by law.

Changes to This Notice

We may update this notice from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the App and this website.
